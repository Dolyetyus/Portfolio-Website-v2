Language Learning Flashcards App

Developed a Java-based desktop language learning flashcards app with a team of 4, focusing on design patterns, software designing with UML, and scrum during the early development processes; OOP, interactive GUI design made using JavaFX, customisable flashcards, game-like elements, achievements and score system, and cultural insights in development. Also integrated JSON processing for data persistence, allowing users to import/export their learning materials.

  • Customizable Flashcards: Edit your learning experience by creating, editing, and managing flashcards or levels.
  • Game-Like Learning: Score points, earn achievement badges, and challenging levels to stay motivated.
  • Listening and Completion Exercises: Improve your listening skills with listening exercises powered by AI.
  • JSON Export/Import: Share and build upon your learning materials with JSON support.
  • Cultural Insights: Learn Dutch idioms, trivia, and fun facts to immerse yourself in the culture as you progress.
